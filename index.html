I have recently obtained my Bachelor's degree from Xidian University and will soon join the Intelligent Information Retrieval Lab (IIR Lab) from the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence (GSAI), Renmin University of China (RUC), supervised by Prof. Jun Xu. My research interests include Information Retrieval, Recommender System, and Large Language Model. Currently, I am collaborating with Huawei AppGallery. My primary focus is on leveraging the latent reasoning capabilities of LLMs to improve the efficiency of multi-agent communications and generative bundle recommendation. I am always open to collaboration and discussions in these areas. If you are interested in, please feel free to reach out!

PS: I am actively seeking academic or industry opportunities. If you are interested in me, please feel free to contact me.
